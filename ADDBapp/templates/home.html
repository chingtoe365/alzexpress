AlzExpress is a database to provide microarray data for the research on neuro-degenerative diseases, mainly focusing on Alzhiemer's disease at current stage. (Parkinson's disease data will be integrated soon!)

It provides the functionality of query on biomolecule, such as RNA, protein, and provides reference for researchers in this field to identify differentially expressed molecules in different categories of samples, with high statistically significance.

It also provides meta-analysis for separate results accross different studies, which help take varying statistical power from different different studies into consideration. Meta-analysis serves to generalize and improve statitical reliability for each queried feature.

To summarize, AlzExpress provides:

  • Differentially expressed molecules anaylsis in different brain regions
  • Meta-analysis for different categorical groups
